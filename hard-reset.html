How to Hard Reset any Android smartphone!

if your android smartphone is not booting & it just hangs on starting logo or maybe you forgot your pattern, pin code or your password, you don't have to go to Care-Center or a mobile repairing shop to fix it, you can give a try at your home  because Many times these problems can be fixed just by hard resetting.

 

Note - During this trick all your apps & phone contacts (sim contacts will be safe).

Lets Do it...

1. First shutdown your smartphone & remove your SIM cards & SD card.

 

2.Now Hold [ Power key + vol up key ] together to enter into Recovery Mode

(if this combination doesn’t works with your device than try other combinations

 given below)

---------------------

Vol. up + Vol down + Power Key (generally works with Micromax)

Vol. down + Power key

Vol. Up + Home key (for Samsung)

---------------------

 

3. if it gives you option to choose Recovery mode or factory mode - choose Recovery mode.

4. Now a screen (similar to the upper image) will appear (if not then press power key or try pressing all keys including touch keys).

5. Now select “ wipe data/factory reset ” by pressing volume up & down keys.

(I will recommend volume up key because sometimes Vol. down key as OK key)

 

6. Now press power key ( if power key doesn’t works then press Vol. down key or

home key in Samsung or try by pressing all touch keys).

7. Now select  “ Yes - delete all user data “ & press your Ok key (power key or Vol down key).

8. Now you will see a screen something like upper image. wait till it ends.

 

9.Now if your smartphone doesn’t restart itself then select reboot now option & press your Ok key (Power key or Vol down Key).

 

Note - Your device will take a little more time than usual to boot up (Start up) after resetting.
